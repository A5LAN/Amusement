Take your Amusement on the go

Relax and get rid of your stress levels on the go.

Driving through Downtown Traffic.

The Amusement app will give you advice to keep you calm. It will also play music according to your mood swings. If stressed out mellow music will be played through your car's audio system.

Driving through long road trips.

Long road trips can be fun if you have others with you but once alone it could take forever. The Amusement app will keep you up and driving as long as possible. WARNING this does not mean you could drive even though you are sleep deprived it only means it will play music to keep you up as you get sleepy.
