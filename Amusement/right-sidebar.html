Spotify History

Here you can check the music that you have listened to on Spotify. You can also use this to make playlist of the songs that had helped you in both stressful situations or late night long driving.

Spotify History Chart

As it is listed above you can see that the "genres" are listed as hyped, mellow, and neutral. The tittle Mellow goes under many genres such as Chill-out, Classical, Slow Hiphop and many more. Meanwhile Hyped goes under EDM, Trance, Euro Electro, and many more. Lastly Neutral goes under the songs or albums or artists you constantly listen to on your own time using spotify.

Driving through Downtown Traffic.

The Amusement app will give you advice to keep you calm. It will also play music according to your mood swings. If stressed out mellow music will be played through your car's audio system.

Driving through long road trips.

Long road trips can be fun if you have others with you but once alone it could take forever. The Amusement app will keep you up and driving as long as possible. WARNING this does not mean you could drive even though you are sleep deprived it only means it will play music to keep you up as you get sleepy.
